Running

11 x 26.2 miles - so far!

Yes, I am one of those people that makes running a part of my personality. I spend a lot of time doing it!

I've been running since I was 11 years old when my dad took us to the park near our house to do hill repeats in the grass. (Side note: it's amazing looking back to see how endurance grows over time. A mile used to feel like the ultimate distance run as a kid!)

I kept running, and the distance kept growing, and since 2018 I've been trying to run one or two marathons a year. Everyone has their own ways of keeping fit, this just happens to be one of mine.
